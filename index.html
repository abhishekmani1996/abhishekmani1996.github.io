Fintech · Recommendation Engine

Fynn - Credit Card Advisor

Problem

Most credit card comparison tools are affiliate-driven and rank cards by signup bonuses, not by real-world fit for the user's spending patterns.

Approach

Built a deterministic four-factor scoring model: spend fit × goal alignment × fee efficiency × eligibility. Inputs are collected in under 2 minutes, and 80+ cards are evaluated in under 1 second.

Outcome

  • Generates a shortlist of 3-5 cards with projected annual value and application links.
  • Includes a score-lift playbook to help users improve approval odds.
  • Offers optional read-only spend analysis for long-term card strategy.
